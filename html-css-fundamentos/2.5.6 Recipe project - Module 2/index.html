Recipe de Strogonoff

This is going to be my favorite recipe.

List of Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/3 cup mustard
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • straw potato to taste

Preparation Mode

  1. In a pan, mix the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter and brown the onion.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken until golden.
  4. Add the mushrooms, ketchup and mustard.
  5. Add the cream and remove from the heat before boiling.
  6. Serve with white rice and potatoes chips.

Additional Information

                especially served at parties. It didn't take long, the stroganoff arrived on everyone's plates.

Recipe de Pancakes

Pasta ingredients list

  • 1 cup of wheat flours
  • 1 glass of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of corn starch
  • 2 units of egg
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of soy oil
  • 1 tablespoon of grated cheese

Chicken stuffing ingredients list

  • 300 g cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1/2 piece of chopped onion
  • 1 unit of minced garlic
  • 1 pcs chopped skinless and seeded tomato
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of parsley
  • Ground peppercorns , ground to taste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 can of canned corn
  • 1/2 can of peas
  • 1/2 cup of cottage cheese

    • Pasta preparation method

    1. Mix everything in the blender and let it rest for about 15 minutes before starting to fry.
    2. Try to use a non-stick frying pan of 18 to 25 cm maximum and with low edges with a drizzle of olive oil, always on medium heat.
    3. Wipe off the excess oil with a paper towel and always pass this paper towel every two fried pancakes.
    4. Always let the skillet heat up for about 30 seconds before adding another amount of batter to fry.
    5. Add enough batter to make thin pancakes and always the same amount from beginning to end.
    6. When the dough has bubbles, use a slotted spoon to remove the whole pancake first and then turn it over.
    7. After all the pancakes are fried, just fill to taste: chicken, hearts of palm, ricotta or meat, put your favorite sauce on the pancakes and serve.

    Filling preparation method

    1. Sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil and over low heat.
    2. When the onion is transparent, add the tomato and sauté for another five minutes.
    3. Add the chicken and corn and season with pepper and adjust salt if necessary.
    4. Add 1/4 cup of water, mix well and, after 3 minutes, turn off the heat.
    5. Mix the curd, parsley and peas and fill the pancakes.

    Recipe de Strogonoff

    List of Ingredients

    • 4 units of egg
    • 1 cup (tea) of sugar
    • 1 glass of milk
    • 2 1/2 pieces of french bread

    Preparation Mode

    1. Beat all the ingredients in the blender and pour into a well caramelized form.
    2. Bake in a preheated oven at 180ºC, in a bain-marie, for about 1h30m

    CyberCook Tips

    1. To make the caramel sauce, place 1 cup of sugar in a saucepan over low heat. When the sugar is golden add 1/2 cup of water and do not stir anymore. The point is when when picking up the syrup with a spoon, a consistent thread runs out and not too thin.
    2. The bain-marie in the oven must not contain too much water because when boiling it may spill into the pudding. Then calculate a maximum of two fingers high in relation to the shape of the pudding.
    3. If you cover the pan with aluminum foil, it will take longer to bake, but you will have a creamier pudding.
    4. The oven time may vary, in addition to the oven, on the size of the pan being used. The higher the dough is in the pan, the longer it will take to bake.
    5. If you want to leave your pudding without that egg taste, pass the yolks through a sieve, without using a spoon, so the yolk film will stay in the sieve.
    6. You can prepare this pudding without the blender, of course, breaking the bread into very small pieces and using a fouet to mix everything very well.
